Features

Our innovative product, Gantavya, offers a variety of distinct features. Below, you'll find a selection of them.

Faster Payment

Dynamic QR code is updated each time, so user can just scan and pay!

Your Face Your Pass

Pay through your face utilizing the technology of Facial-recognition.

Scalability and Future Expansion

The project's modular design allows for scalability and the addition of new features or improvements in the future.

Real Time Tracking

Passengers can track the real-time location of buses using their smartphones.

Price Transparency

The project calculates fares based on the actual distance traveled by the bus.

Security

Having Camera make people wary of there surrondings. So, the bus can also be secured and protected.
